FRAME NUMBERS EXPLANATION:

T107-228503-IW3 = Track 107, Frame 228503, Swath IW3
T005-009414-IW1 = Track 5, Frame 9414, Swath IW1  
T005-009413-IW1 = Track 5, Frame 9413, Swath IW1

What each number means:
- TRACK = Orbital path (like highway route)
- FRAME = Geographic tile/scene (like street address)  
- SWATH = Radar beam section (IW1, IW2, IW3)

Frame 228503 Coverage:
- Shows COMPLETE Ala Archa glacier
- Covers ~250km x 250km ground area
- Same geographic footprint every orbit
- Available every 12 days on Track 107

Why 228503 is special for your glacier:
- Frame 228503 is perfectly positioned over Ala Archa
- Other frame numbers (like 9413, 9414) only show PARTS of glacier  
- Frame 228503 = "address" where your whole glacier lives
- Every year, same frame number = same geographic coverage

Frame = Fixed geographic "tile" that satellite photographs
Each frame has permanent boundaries on Earth's surface
